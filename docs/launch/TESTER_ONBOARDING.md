# Tester Onboarding

Welcome to the Schede alpha. Read this once before installing the app.
It takes 4 minutes and saves the team a lot of time.

---

## What we are testing

This is an early alpha of an adaptive workout app. We are validating:

1. Whether the app generates **useful** workouts.
2. Whether you keep coming back the second and third week.
3. Whether the workout screen works **in the gym** (sweat, gloves, one hand).

We are **not** testing visual polish, copy, or marketing material. Skip
that feedback for now.

---

## Install

### iPhone
1. You should have received a TestFlight invite email. Tap **Accept**.
2. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store.
3. Open the invite again and tap **Install Schede**.

### Android
1. Open the Play Internal Testing invite link from your invite email.
2. Tap **Become a tester**.
3. Open Play Store → Schede → **Install**.

If updates appear, install them. Always run the latest build.

---

## First-time setup

1. Sign up with email + password.
2. Complete onboarding (it asks about goal, days/week, equipment, injuries).
3. Hit **Start today’s workout**.
4. Do at least one full set, even if light. Adherence data starts here.

You can sign in on **one device at a time** during alpha.

---

## What we expect from you

You signed up to test, not just install. Concretely:

- Do **at least 3 workouts per week** for **4 weeks**.
- Log every set, including warmups (single-tap the set, no need to type).
- Open the app at least once on the rest days.
- Fill the **weekly survey** (one minute, link in-app under Profile).
- Send bug reports through the channels in the next section.

If you cannot commit to that, please tell us now — we will pass the slot
to someone on the waitlist.

---

## How to report a bug

Pick the channel that matches what happened.

### A. The app crashed or froze

- iOS: TestFlight will offer to "Share screenshot and feedback". Tap **Send**.
- Android: open the app again, send us a message in the Telegram group
  with what you were doing in the 10 seconds before the crash.

### B. Something looked wrong but the app kept working

- Open the alpha **Telegram group** and post:
  - What you were doing.
  - What you expected.
  - What you saw (screenshot helps).
  - Phone model + OS version if possible.

### C. You think the workout was bad

- Use the **Feedback** entry under Profile → "Tell us about your workout".
- One sentence is enough. We read all of them.

We **prefer one short report per issue** over long catch-up messages. If
you find five issues, send five reports.

---

## Feedback we want, and feedback to skip

### Want
- Anything about the workout screen feeling slow, awkward, or annoying.
- Sync issues (you logged a set, it vanished, doubles, wrong order).
- Anything that broke offline / in a bad-signal gym.
- Workouts that felt wrong for your level (too hard, too easy, too long).
- Confusion during onboarding — which screen, what you didn’t understand.

### Skip
- Color palette / icon style / branding.
- "Add this feature" — we are not collecting feature requests yet.
- General product strategy ideas.

If you have feature ideas, jot them down somewhere — we will collect them
in beta, not in alpha.

## Privacy and data

By participating in the alpha you consent to:

- Crash reports being sent to our error tracker (Sentry).
- Anonymous product analytics being sent (event names, screens, timings).
  We do not capture set values, body measurements, or PRs in analytics.
- Your workouts being stored on Supabase for the app to function.

You can:

- Opt out of analytics in Profile → Privacy.
- Request **full account deletion** at any time. Account deletion removes
  your auth user, profile, workout history, and analytics records linked
  to your `user_id` within 14 days.
- Ask to see your data — same path, contact support.

See the in-app **Privacy** screen for the full notice.

## Weekly survey

You will receive a **weekly survey** (Sunday evening, 6 questions, ~1 min):

1. How many workouts did you complete this week?
2. Did any workout feel wrong (too hard / too easy / too long / boring)?
3. Did you have any sync or crash issue?
4. Did you skip any workout? Why?
5. On a 0–10 scale, how likely are you to keep using the app next week?
6. Anything else?

This is mandatory while you are in the alpha.

---

## Optional interview

Some of you will be invited to a **30-minute video call** in week 3 or 4.
We share screen, walk through the app together, and ask what felt wrong.
This is optional but extremely valuable; if you can, please accept.
